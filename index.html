About Me

I'm a Finnish-American software engineer best known for creating the Linux kernel and the Git version control system. What started as a personal project in 1991 has grown into the foundation of modern computing infrastructure.

My philosophy is simple: write good code, be direct in communication, and let technical merit speak for itself. I believe in the power of open source and collaborative development to solve complex problems.

When I'm not maintaining the kernel or reviewing patches, I enjoy diving, spending time with my family, and occasionally engaging in spirited technical debates on the Linux Kernel Mailing List.
